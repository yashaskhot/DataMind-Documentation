Datamind Usage Docs

Requirements

In order to use Datamind, you will need a modern web browser and an internet connection. There are no other requirements.

CSV File Requirements

The CSV file you want to process with My Project must have the following columns:

  • sid: Student ID's
  • stream: Stream of Students.
  • name: Name of Students
  • mob_no: Mobile Number of Students
  • email: Email of Students.
  • gender: Gender of Student
  • x_perc: Xth Percentage of Students.
  • xii_perc: XIIth Percentage of Students.
  • cet_perc: CET Percentile of Students

 

  • father_occupation: Occupation of Father
  • mother_name: Name of Mother
  • mother_occupation: Occupation of Mother
  • parent_income: Income of Parent
  • pincode: Pincode of Students Residence
  • year: Student Admission Year
  • sector: Sector of Parents Jobs
  • quota: Whether student is of minority quota or not.
  • jee_perc: JEE Percentile of Student
  • father_name: Name of Father.
  • physics_xii: Physics Percentage of Student in XIIth
  • chem_xii: Chemistry Percentage of Student in XIIth.
  • math_xii: Mathematics Percentage of Student in XIIth.

Uploading the CSV File

To upload your CSV file, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Upload CSV" button/drop your csv file in the box.
  2. Click "Submit" to upload your CSV file.

Processing the CSV File

Once your CSV file has been uploaded, Datamind will process it and provide you with the results if your csv file fullfils the criteria.
